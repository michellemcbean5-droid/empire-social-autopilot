# Privacy Policy — Empire Social Autopilot

**Last Updated:** July 8, 2026

## Introduction

Empire Social Autopilot ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, profile picture (optional)
- **Payment Information**: Processed by Apple App Store, Google Play, or RevenueCat (we do not store card details)
- **Social Media Accounts**: Platform connections (Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok) with your authorization

### Usage Data
- **Content Data**: Topics, brand voice settings, generated posts, scheduled content calendars
- **Analytics**: App usage patterns, feature engagement, crash reports
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version, app version, unique device identifiers

### AI Processing Data
- **Content Inputs**: Topics and prompts you provide for AI content generation
- **Generated Outputs**: AI-created captions, hashtags, and recommendations
- **Competitor Data**: Public social media handles you choose to analyze

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Service Provision**: Generate, schedule, and publish social media content
2. **AI Improvement**: Train and improve our content generation models (anonymized)
3. **Analytics**: Understand usage patterns to improve the app
4. **Monetization**: Process subscriptions and in-app purchases
5. **Communications**: Send push notifications about post status and insights
6. **Security**: Detect fraud and protect against unauthorized access

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- Content calendars stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage
- Sync data stored on secure cloud servers
- We use industry-standard security practices

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |

| HuggingFace | AI content generation | Content prompts (anonymized) |
| RevenueCat | Subscription management | Purchase history |

## Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Portability**: Export your data in JSON format
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics or delete account

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@qempireai.com

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Analytics**: Anonymized after 12 months
- **Backups**: Retained for 90 days for disaster recovery

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via app notification or email.
